Pricing Logic

This page explains exactly how each line item total is calculated.

Back to Bid Form

Single Source Formula

row_total = qty * ((base_price + difficulty_add[level]) * toggle_multiplier * manual_multiplier)

Meaning

`difficulty_add[level]` is an absolute dollar add per UOM (not a percent).

Example: +$3.00 at level 2 and qty 50 adds $150.00 to that row before toggles/multiplier.

Grand Total

`grand_total = sum(row_total for all line items)`
